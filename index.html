places must visit

prathamesh resort

i had visited in march 2021

Prathamesh Resorts is always ready to welcome you with open arms. Situated at the serene surroundings of Khed Shivapur, this established family resort near Pune is only an hour’s drive away from the city. Prathamesh Resorts provides you peace and relaxation on a platter. You can unwind yourself and enjoy stress-free time with family, friends, relatives and even office colleagues. The multi cuisine restaurant at Prathamesh Resorts will tickle your taste buds and satisfy them with rich taste and high quality nutritious food which is extremely essential for a healthy lifestyle.

platinum 52

i had visited in november 2020

The most relaxing scenic and wonderful home away from home surrounded by the misty Mountain Ranges of the Bhor Ghats. Ideal vacation settings around nature at it's best with the opportunity to enjoy the flora and Fauna of the Wonderful Sahyadris. Enjoy the delicious Food Cooked by the locals with freshly harvested vegetables and local spices which will tickle your palate.Tastefully done cottages with modern amenities and most of all friendly services provided by our well trained staff. Come and enjoy nature like you never have before.
